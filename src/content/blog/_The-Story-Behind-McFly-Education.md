> Every great flight school begins with **a story of passion and persistence**.

## Early Beginnings

- Marty attended a **summer camp at 13**, sparking a lifelong love for aviation.
- After college, he rediscovered flying in his **early 20s** and decided to turn passion into profession.

## Using Social Media to Learn and Share

- Started **Instagram** to document training and prepare for the CFI checkride.
- Engaged with peers for **feedback and revisions**, creating a collaborative learning approach.

## Founding McFly Education

- Emphasis on **mentorship, safety, and community**.
- One-on-one instruction and **hands-on control** became core principles.
- Focused on **building a pilot community**, not just students.

## Values That Guide the School

- Goal-oriented and **people-first** approach.
- Community service and **student engagement**.
- Safety is always the **top priority**.

## Ready to join the community?

McFly Education isn’t just a school — it’s a **mentorship-driven hub** where aspiring pilots learn, grow, and fly safely.
