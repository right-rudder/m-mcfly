> Choosing the right airport can make **your flight training safer, more efficient, and more enjoyable**.

## Cable Airport (KCCB)

- Located in Upland, CA.
- Ideal for **student pilots** with less congested traffic.
- Offers access to diverse training airspace and **one-on-one instruction**.

## Brackett Field (POC)

- Supports **cross-country flight practice**.
- Larger airspace and controlled tower for advanced students.

## Chino Airport (CNO)

- Offers both GA and experimental aircraft operations.
- Great for practicing **complex patterns and emergency procedures**.

## Tips for Airport Selection

- Check **airspace class** and traffic density.
- Ensure **student-friendly services**: FBOs, instructors, fuel availability.
- Prioritize **hands-on instruction opportunities**.

## Ready to train locally?

Picking the right airports enhances **learning, safety, and flight exposure**, helping you become a confident pilot faster.
