> Your first flight lesson is the moment **learning takes off** , literally.

## Pre-Flight Briefing

- Review the flight plan and objectives.
- Discuss basic **cockpit controls**.
- Safety demonstration and emergency procedures overview.

## Hands-On Flying

- Take **early control of the aircraft** with guidance.
- Focus on **basic maneuvers**: straight-and-level, climbs, descents, turns.
- Apply real-time feedback from your instructor.

## Post-Flight Debrief

- Highlight strengths and areas to improve.
- Compare lesson objectives vs. actual performance.
- Set objectives for the next flight.

## Tips for a Smooth First Lesson

- Dress comfortably and wear appropriate footwear.
- Stay **hydrated and rested**.
- Ask questions , curiosity accelerates learning.

## Ready for takeoff?

Your first flight sets the foundation. Embrace the experience and focus on **hands-on skill development**.
